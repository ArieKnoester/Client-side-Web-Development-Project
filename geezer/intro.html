Welcome to Geezer Games!

Our Goal:
Here at Geezer Games (GG) we will be giving a ‘first impressions’ review of PC platform video games. For our first review, we will be covering The Legend of Grimrock 2. Hover your mouse pointer over the menu above to check it out!

Are you ready for The Legend of Grimrock 2?

What does first impressions mean?
Due to a busy schedule and a strict publication deadline; we simply don’t have the time to play the games reviewed to completion. Upon publication of our first review, approximately 23 hours was put into playing The Legend of Grimrock 2. We believe this is enough time to write a thorough first impressions review.

What rating system do we use?
We don’t. We will simply state our opinions and experience with the game and it will be up to the reader to use the information given to decide if it is a game they will want to play.

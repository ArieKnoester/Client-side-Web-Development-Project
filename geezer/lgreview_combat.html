The Legend of Grimrock 2

Combat:

Defend yourself from all mannar of creature!

For those who’ve played the first game in the series, combat will be very familiar. Weapons equipped on your characters will appear in their respective portraits. Attacks are made by clicking on the weapon. You’ll want your melee characters in the front row with ranged attackers in the back. It is possible to melee from the back row, but you’ll need to spend a few attribute points before you can. We recommend a stick-and-move strategy instead of standing toe to toe with enemies. Especially in the beginning when you don’t have good weapons and equipment. Be mindful of your environment. When facing off against multiple opponents you can easily get boxed in leaving your easily killed back row of characters exposed.

Puzzles:

Just like the first game, there are plenty of delicious puzzles to solve and secrets to find. The pacing of them is pretty close to perfect. The puzzles and secrets break up the combat of the game and provide a nice change of pace preventing the game from becoming too repetitive. At the start they won’t be too challenging, but as you progress through the game they will become much more difficult. Sometimes you’ll get hints. Sometimes the hints are located in a completely different area than the puzzle. Other times you won’t get a hint at all leaving you scratching your head as to what to do.

Can you spot the hidden button in this wall?

Secrets:

While finding secret areas isn’t necessary for game completion, We recommend keeping a vigilant eye out for them. Not only are they fun, but you’ll be rewarded with items that will help you survive. Hidden switches, pressure plates, keys to scrounge for, buried chests to find from vague descriptions. It’s all there. Thankfully the mapping system has great functionality. It allows you to place markers and make notes to remind yourself if you need to come back to something later.

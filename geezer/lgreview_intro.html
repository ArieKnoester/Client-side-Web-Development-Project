The Legend of Grimrock 2

Introduction:

Stranded on the mysterious Island of Nex.

Having played and completed the first Legend of Grimrock game, I went into the sequel with high hopes. I can say that I am not disappointed. If you are not familiar with the series, the games are a modern take on old school RPG dungeon crawlers. The first game in the series was received well by both critics and players and breathed new life into a genre that had been thought long dead. The Legend of Grimrock 2 was released on Oct 15, 2014. The series was developed by Almost Human, a four person independent company based in Finland.

Like in the original, The Legend of Grimrock 2 starts you off as 4 prisoners. However, instead of being sealed off into a mountain dungeon for crimes against the king, your group is being transported by ship to an unknown location. The ship soon wrecks and your cage washes up on the island of Nex.

Another taunting letter from the Island Master.


After escaping your confinement you soon find yourself in a hostile environment with only sticks, rocks, and bare hands to fight with. As you make your way inland, you discover a letter (one of many to follow) from the mysterious, Island Master, who seems to take delight in seeing whether you will survive. You will explore. Fight. Find hidden items, secret areas, and solve avoid perilous traps. You will solve puzzles and riddles... whatever it takes until your final confrontation with the Island Master.
